Project Overview

Individual Course Project
Duration: 4 months, Sep 2019 - Dec 2019
Tools: Sketch, Invision Studio, Adobe Illustrator
Methodologies: Double Diamond, Reflective Design, QOC, Brainstorm, Survey & Interview


The project is about a community-based social network system affiliated to Facebook, which will make the potential user directly enter the system without creating a new account. The system would bring users together to create a strong community, encourage them to get involved in social activities, combine their community life with school life, help them arrange time in a better way, and find people who share the same interest. 

BrainStorming

I brought the finding of the user research and the sketches to the brainstorming session to discuss with my classmates. and together we brainstorm new ideas to find possible solutions. These suggestions really helped me a lot in the following design prototyping. Below is the mindmap that shows the features of the product I wanted to implement according to the brainstorming discussion. 

Paper Prototype

My final design is a community-based social network system affiliated to Facebook, which will make the potential user directly enter the system without creating a new account. the system would bring users together to create a strong community, encourage them to get involved in social activities, combine their community life with school life, help them arrange time in a better way, and find people who share the same interest. The whole system is divided into 5 different parts -- Dashboard, Group Chat, Activity, Collaboration, and Mini-Game
