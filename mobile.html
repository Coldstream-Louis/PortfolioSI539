Project Overview

Google Design Exercise
Individual Project
Duration: 7 Days
Tools: Sketch, Adobe Illustrator, Invision


Help the Facilities and Operations of the university to improve the upkeep of campus facilities by creating a new system for reporting any facilities that may need maintenance or repair. Analyze the pros and cons of the current system, and then design an experience that allows students to report building or equipment issues on campus more efficiently. 

User Research Findings

Generally speaking, the current system, which was just updated last year, has many good design points. It has another internal system that helps the facilities managers and F&O staff members contact each other directly, updating the information and status of the orders. The staff member I interviewed told me that this system works well most of the time. 

BrainStorming

According to the findings summarized in the previous step, I started to brainstorm the potential solutions to solve the shortcomings of the FIX-IT system. After that, I analyzed those ideas one by one for each problems to determine which one of them should be implemented in the prototype.
