Project Overview

The research goal of College of Arts and Communication of Tongji University is to investigate the inquiry learning evaluation and learner test environment in virtual reality environment, aiming at the experimental teaching environment of middle school students with virtual and reality integration, and establish a multi-level and multi-channel behavior collection and analysis environment, combined with specific learning content for middle school students, research experiments. The environment and experimental methods enable the current analysis of learning behavior to effectively combine historical learning behavior data, thereby obtaining more accurate conclusions about the learning behavior attributes, psychological attributes and physiological attributes of learners. 

Therefore, the research group of the college decided to develop a testing system firstly, to do the user test and collect data about users' behavior. This project is exactly to develop such a test system -- using VR technology to build a virtual chemistry world, let students explore freely in it, and make full use of the immersive characteristics of VR's fully immersive environment to stimulate students' interest in chemistry inquiry.

This project is developed using MVC (Model-View-Controller) model. However, the structure of Unity's MVC model is slightly different from the well-known MVC pattern structure of ordinary application development.


The core hardware devices used in this project are Kinect, Microsoft's somatosensory interactive device, and Oculus Rift, Oculus VR's head-mounted virtual reality device. The development companies of both devices have released free Unity Developer Kits and SDKs, which integrates several basic interactive functions very well. They are the fundamental of the whole system.
