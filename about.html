About Me

Here is a short introduction about my academic life

Hi, welcome to visit my personal pages. I am now a graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information.


When I was a freshman at my former university, I firstly major in mechanical and electronic. But not for long, I found I was interested in computer science and software development. Afterward, I switched to Software Engineering, and that’s why I need to take one more year of basic courses after my freshman year. But I never regretted it, due to my great thirst of absorbing knowledge from what I’m interested in.


However, coding is not my whole life in the undergraduate study, I also have other interests. I like design because I think it is the basis of solving problems, and it does exist everywhere in our life. I like to communicate with users, listening to their requirements and advice, sharing our creativity to come up with a better solution. I like to involve in multi-disciplinary projects, cooperating with people from different fields, to see the miraculous spark from the collision of different ideas and thinking modes. Human-Computer Interaction, which contains all of my interests, would undoubtedly be my best major. 


That's why I come to UMSI and choose to study in the UX/HCI track. I have learned some essential methodologies and rationals about user experience research and design here, and will learn more in the future. I am looking forward to improving my skills in interaction design and user research to create products with better user experience in the future work.
